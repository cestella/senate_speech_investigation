With the national debt at a record $13.7 trillion and rising every day, Senator Bob Corker will be participating in a series of town hall meetings to address America’s debt crisis.  Corker will deliver a sobering slide presentation on Washington’s spending trends and mounting debt levels and then respond to questions from the audience.

A complete schedule of the events, which are open to the public, is listed below.  To learn more about America’s debt crisis visit: http://corker.senate.gov/public/index.cfm?p=america-s-debt-crisis
