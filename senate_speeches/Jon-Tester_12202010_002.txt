(U.S. SENATE) – Senator Jon Tester today released the following statement after the U.S. Senate unanimously confirmed Darrell Bell to serve as Montana’s next U.S. Marshal:
“Darrell’s got the drive and experience needed for this job.  He’s fair and respected.  And I look forward to working with him in the years ahead.”
Bell is 30-year veteran of the Billings Police Department.
