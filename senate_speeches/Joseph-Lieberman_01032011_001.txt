WASHINGTON, DC – Senator Joe Lieberman (I-CT) today issued the following statement in memoriam of former Connecticut Congressman William Ratchford of Danbury:

“Bill Ratchford dedicated his life to improving the lives of others. I have never worked with a more honorable, decent, and effective public servant. From our early days in the state legislature through his career of service and advocacy, Bill was a good man who got some very good things done for the people he represented. Hadassah and I extend our prayers and condolences to Barbara and their loved ones.”
