WASHINGTON, DC-- Senator Joe Lieberman (I-CT) today congratulated the UCONN women's basketball program following its 88th consecutive win:

"It is with great joy that I congratulate the Lady Huskies on their amazing record-run of 88 straight victories,” Lieberman said. “The Lady Huskies, led by their remarkable coach Geno Auriemma, continue to make Connecticut proud with their countless accomplishments."
