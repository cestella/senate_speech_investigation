1:30 p.m. – Senator Patrick Leahy, Senator Bernie Sanders and a representative of Congressman Peter Welch will recognize 29 young Vermonters who are being nominated to United States Service Academies to be members of the Class of 2015.  At the annual reception in the Cedar Creek Room of the Vermont Statehouse, nominees from throughout Vermont will receive nomination certificates signed by each member of the Vermont Congressional Delegation.

The names of this year’s nominees, selected by Leahy, Sanders and Welch through a competitive vetting process, and forwarded to the Academies for final decisions, are:
