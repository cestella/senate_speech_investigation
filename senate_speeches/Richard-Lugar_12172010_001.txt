U.S. Sen. Dick Lugar is scheduled to appear on This Week on Sunday, December 19, 2010, to discuss the New START treaty, the Obama administration's Afghanistan war strategy, and the impasse on funding the government.
 
The show airs from 10:00 a.m. to 11:00 a.m. EST. The entire episode will be available at http://abcnews.go.com/thisweek.
