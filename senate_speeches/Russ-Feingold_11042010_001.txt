 U.S. Senator Russ Feingold released the following statement regarding the decision by the United States Navy to request more ships from Wisconsin shipbuilder Marinette Marine.  The Navy is proposing to purchase 10 Littoral Combat Ships (LCS) from the northeastern Wisconsin company, which would support jobs in northeastern Wisconsin and throughout the state.  

“I am pleased the Navy would like to purchase more ships from Marinette Marine.  This is good news for northeastern Wisconsin and I was proud to support Marinette Marine in its bid to build these ships.”
