U.S. Senator Susan Collins, a member of the Senate Armed Services and Appropriations Committees, today announced that the Naval Sea Systems Command has awarded Bath Iron Works (BIW) an $8,494,937 contract modification for additional work on the DDG-1000 destroyer.  

This modification will provide for additional systems engineering services associated with the detail design and construction of the DDG-1000 by BIW, which is the lead yard contractor of the DDG-1000.  Work will be performed in Bath, and is expected to be completed by September 2011.
